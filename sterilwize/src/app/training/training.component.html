This allows SterilWize to identify each barcoded set of instruments from the beginning of sterilization through to the patient appointment where these instruments are used. Reports are generated in which each instrument set is associated with all of its related data such as:

  • Date & time of sterilization
  • Cycle number with Temperature & pressure data
  • Operator
  • Sterilizer
  • BI test
  • BD test
  • EI test

When used with “Smart” sterilizers & “BI” incubators data is automatically collected and compiled into reports.

Therefore, at any time in the future, you will easily be able to determine which set of instruments were used on a specific patient, on a specific date, and have all related data related to that particular set of instruments.

Additional SterilWize features include step by step reference videos and information to help staff members ensure that all steps necessary for compliance are followed.

Required IPAC Manuals, Equipment manuals, RCDSO, CDHO & Public Health Requirements & reference manuals are also available through this system.
